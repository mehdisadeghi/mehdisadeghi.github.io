Watch DRM-Protected Content on Debian Jessie

Sun, Jun 14, 2015 - Read in 3 Min
I have recently installed Debian 8.0 (codenamed Jessie) and like many other distros I have to gradually fix problems that I came across. Here is how I managed to watch Amazon’s prime videos using Iceweasel. Trying to watch Amazon’s videos which are DRM-protected results in an error pages in Iceweasel on Debian. Flash is installed on my machine but DRM content on Flash requires HAL to play. The problem is that HAL is deprecated and is not used anymore by major distributions including Debian.
